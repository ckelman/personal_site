The Sirge

Go to Site

A fully functioning news aggregation site with 20,000 users, built with Node.js and Express. The Sirge pulls articles from all over the web and makes suggestions to users based on the articles they have read in the past. The site also allows users to share, like, and save articles for later. Now maintained by Cogo Labs.
